Buff Sukuti

Buff Sukuti is a popular Newari dish, it is prepared using dried buffalo meat It is one of the favorite food of the Newari community. The dish is quite easy to prepare, lets follow along.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20-25 min.

serving

3 servings

instructions

step 1

Heat oil in a pan.

step 2

Shred the dried buff meat into small piceces and fry the meat in the pan for 5-7 minutes.

step 3

Chop Onion and tomato into small cubes and mince Green chilly.

step 4

Mix the chopped Onion, tomatoes and chili paste with Vinegar.

step 5

Add Salt, Cumin powder, red chili powder and chopped coriander and mix it well.

step 6

Add the fried buff meat few minutes before serving.

step 7

Sadheko Sukuti is ready to be served.

ingredients

200 grams Dried Buff meat

1 medium sized Onion

3 pcs of green chilly

1 tsp of red chili powder

2 tbsp Vinegar

Salt per taste

1/2 tsp of Cumin

20 grams Coriander leaves

2 tsp Oil

tools

Cooking Pan (Non-stick/Cast iron)

Stapula/Spoon

Knife
