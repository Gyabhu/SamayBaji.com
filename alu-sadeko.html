Alu Sadheko

Nepali Alu Sadeko is a popular Newari dish / pickle. It is a favorite food of the Newari community. You can be easily seen making aloo Sadeko in every Newari event in Nepal. You can easily find and eat alu Sadeko in any restaurant inside the valley It is made from boiled potatoes and roasted Tomatoes mixed with spices. The dish is quite easy to prepare, lets follow along.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

5-10 min.

serving

4-5 servings

instructions

step 1

Boil 3-4 medium sized Potatoes, after boiling peel and cut potatoes into desireable pieces.

step 2

Add Onion,Tomato,Green Chili,Red-Chili powder, coriander powder and mix it well.

step 3

Now add Oil, ginger & garlic and mix it well. You Can also add lemon juice after this step.

step 4

Heat a pan, add 1tbsp of cooking oil.

step 5

Add Methi( Fenugreek seeds) after the pan is piping hot. When the seeds turn black, turn off the heat and add Turmeric powder.

step 6

Add this mixture to the potato mix.

step 7

Mix it well, sprinkle Coriander leaves and Alu Sadheko is ready to serve!

ingredients

500 grams of Red Potato

1 Small sized Onion

Garlic Cloves and Ginger paste

1/2tsp Turmeric Powder, Methi(Fenugreek),Cumin powder

2 tbsp Mustard oil

Salt

1 pcs Lemon

20 grams Coriander leaves

tools

Cooking Pan (Non-stick/Cast iron)

Stapula/Spoon

Pressure Cooker

Medium sized Mixing Bowl
