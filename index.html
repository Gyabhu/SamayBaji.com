SamayBaji

Samay Baji is an authentic traditional dish of Newar community in Nepal. In recent years this food has become one of the main attractions of Nepal. It is also commonly known as Newari Khaja set. This dish has been passed down from many generations and is still much appreciated by the people. It carries unique culture and cooking techniques that are no where to be found in Nepal. The taste of Newari dishes can remain in your taste buds for a quite long time, it is definatley a MUST try!

There are no specific seasons for this dish to be served but, mostly served during auspicious occasions. The dish covers a lot of varities and has both non-veg and veg items, it is usually served drinks such as Aila and Chyang. Samay Baji is an integral part of Newari cuisine. It is a traditional and authentic dish that is synonymous to starters or the first meal of a course. It heralds every feast and celebration among Newars. The dish symbolizes good luck, fortune, happiness and longevity. The dish is a platter of various items such as Choila, Kachela, Achar, Bhatmas, Bara and many more. Samay Baji has become popular these days among many restaruants and hotels. Many people outside the Newari commuinty also appreciate and love the goodness of these flavourful dishes. We have covered a lot of items and dishes that are prepared in the platter in our website, please do check it out and enjoy the good food!

A taste of Newari Culture

Newar, is a community who inhabitated inside the kathmadu Valley around mid-1600s. Inside the valley they mainly reside in Kathmandu, Bhaktapur and Patan. Newar community has rich, diverse and their own unique culture. Newars are one of the primary reasons behind valley's rich history arts, architecture and business. Beside the valley, Palpa, Butwal and Bandipur are also highly populated by the Newars.

Newars have their own language called 'Nepal Bhasa', they are primarily involved in business these days but, many Newaris still practice agriculture, arts and trade. Newars participate in lot of the festivals that they call 'Jatras' and in these 'Jatras' food are always one of the intergal ceremonies. To name a few of these 'Jatras' we have 'Indra Jatra', ' Rato Machhindranath Jatra' , 'Gai Jatra' and so on. Newari culture is vast, it carries a huge legacy and also shapes a lot of Nepalese history and culture. Newari Coumminty are one the most famous community to be known in Nepal, they are extremely friendly and welcoming people. Their traditions,values and norms are great, they are hardworking people who also love to celebrate life.
