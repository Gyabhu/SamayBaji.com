Kachela

‘Kachi’ stands for raw and ‘la’ stands for meat in Newari Language. It is also very popular dish which uses raw minced meat which is mixed with heat creating spices and with hand for quite a long time.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

5-10 min.

serving

4-5 servings

instructions

step 1

Take a bowl and add meat, chili powder, cumin powder, chopped onion, garam masala, ginger, garlic, salt and mix it well.

step 2

Heat a pan and add oil, fenugreek seeds, diced coconut and turmeric powder.

step 3

Pour it into mixture and mix it well.

step 4

Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve.

ingredients

200 grams of Minced Buffalo meat

Salt to taste

15 grams of Chili Powder, Cumin Powder and Garam Masala

Chopped Onions

5 grams of Turmeric Powder

40ml Cooked Mustard Oil

Chopped coriander leaves

Ginger Garlic Paste

Methi (Fenugreek Seeds)

tools

Cooking Pan

Stapula

Knife

Medium/Big sized Mixing Bowl
