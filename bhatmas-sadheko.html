Bhatmas Sadheko

Soybean Salad (Bhatmas Sadeko) is a Nepali appetizer that can be enjoyed as a salad or a side with other Nepali dish such as choila and Potato Salad, It is gluten-free and vegan as well, It is a must have dish. The preparation of this dish is quite simple, easy and quick.

prep time

25-30 min.

cook time

5-10 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat and pan fry soybean until they slightly brown. Soybean doesn't require presoaking or boiling.

step 2

Remove soybean from the pan and use paper towel to soak extra oil if desired.

step 3

In a big bowl, combine it with other ingredients and let it sit for hour or so before serving.

ingredients

1 tbsp Mustard Oil

1/2 Chopped Onion

4 Garlic cloves, finely minced

2-3 inch Ginger, peeled and thinly sliced

4-5 green chili, sliced

2 teaspoon schzeuan pepper (Timur, optional)

1 teaspoon turmeric powder

1-2 lemon

2 teaspoon red chili powder

handful green onion and cilantro, chopped

tools

Cooking Pan

Stapula/Spoon

Knife

Medium/Big sized Mixing Bowl
